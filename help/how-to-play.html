How to play

Overview

The goal of the game is to clear the board of all tiles. You can only select tiles that are on the edge and do not have a tile above it, or to left and right.
you can match tiles that look alike, but there are wildcard tiles, the flower and season tiles which can be matched to any other of its own kind.

The Tiles

Bamboo Tiles - range from 1 to 10
Circle Tiles - range from 1 to 10
Character Tiles - range from 1 to 10 and 'c', 'f', 'n', 's', 'w', 'e'
Flower Tiles - Bamboo, Chysanthemum, Orchid and Plum
Season Tiles - Winter, Spring, Summer and Fall
White Dragon Tile - Which is represented by a sparrow

How to Win

The goal is to be able to clear all the tiles, this can be difficult depending on the order in which you select tiles. some choices and lead to a dead end but every board has a solution from beginning to end.

Clear the board of all tiles and you will win and get a delightful surprise!
